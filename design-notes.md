# ForgeOS Customer-Facing Website Design Notes

## Inspiration: stonesystems.io
- **Core Offering**: Review funnel system that generates 5-star reviews
- **Positioning**: Simple, automated, results-focused
- **Tone**: Confident, slightly playful, direct
- **Key Phrases**: "5-Star Magic", "One Click", "Stop worrying"

## ForgeOS Brand Identity
- **Company Name**: ForgeOS (Forge Operating System)
- **Tagline**: "The AI Growth Engine for Local Businesses"
- **Positioning**: Complete AI marketing system, not just one tool
- **Target**: Local service businesses (plumbers, HVAC, dentists, etc.)

## Value Stack (What We Sell)
1. AI-Powered Website + Database Reactivation
2. Conversation AI Bot (24/7 lead qualification)
3. Missed Call Text Back (no lead left behind)
4. Review Automation (5-star funnel)
5. Reactivation Campaigns (recurring revenue)

## Design Direction
- Dark theme with orange/amber accents (matches training hub)
- Clean, minimal, high-contrast
- Large typography for headlines
- Subtle animations on scroll
- Social proof heavy (testimonials, logos, stats)
- Single strong CTA: "Book a Strategy Call"

## Page Structure
1. Hero: Bold headline + subhead + CTA + social proof bar
2. Problem/Pain: "Your competitors are stealing your customers"
3. Solution: The ForgeOS System (visual value stack)
4. Features: 5 core components with icons
5. Results: Case studies / stats
6. Testimonials: 3-4 video or text testimonials
7. Pricing: "Starting at $X/mo" or "Book a Call"
8. FAQ: Common objections handled
9. Footer: Contact, links, legal
